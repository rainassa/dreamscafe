ABOUT THE CAFE

A unique and different place with a theme that relates to people's lives. This concept can create a space for people to feel comfortable, share stories, or simply soak up feelings in a somewhat melancholy, yet hopeful atmosphere.

It is a space for anyone who wants to experience life, share stories, and find beauty in imperfection. A place that not only offers food, but also provides a space for healing, acceptance, and positive change in personal and family life.

"Jika rumahmu berisik, maka datanglah kemari dan anggaplah tempat ini sebagai rumahmu yang lain."

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Baskara Maheswara & Adara Maanavika

Opening hours: everyday from 10am to 10pm.

Address: 23 Askara street, 1010, IDN
